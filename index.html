The Vision

Building the future of personal fitness training through intelligent form analysis

True Form was born from a simple observation: most people working out at home have no way to know if they're performing exercises correctly. Poor form leads to injuries, wasted effort, and frustration. Traditional solutions require expensive personal trainers or gym memberships.

This application uses advanced pose estimation technology to track 33 key body landmarks in real-time, analyzing the angles and positions that determine correct exercise form. The counter only increments when the movement meets the criteriaâ€”ensuring every rep counts.

Currently supporting four foundational bodyweight exercises: push-ups, pull-ups, sit-ups, and squats. Each exercise has been carefully calibrated to detect the nuances of proper form.

Development Journey

From concept to functional prototypeâ€”the steps that shaped this project

Step 01

Ideation & Research

Explored the problem space of home fitness, researched pose estimation technologies, and identified MediaPipe as the optimal solution for real-time skeletal tracking.

Step 02

UI/UX Design

Sketched initial wireframes and designed the app flow. Created a clean, intuitive interface that puts exercise selection and form feedback front and center.

Step 03

Core Architecture

Set up the React Native development environment with Expo Go. Established TypeScript for type safety and integrated MediaPipe's pose detection pipeline.

Step 04

Pose Detection Integration

Implemented real-time camera feed processing, extracted 33 skeletal landmarks, and built the visualization layer to render pose overlays on screen.

Step 05

Form Analysis Algorithm

Developed custom algorithms to calculate joint angles and detect exercise phases. Defined thresholds for correct form across all four exercises.

Step 06

Rep Counter Logic

Built state machine for tracking exercise phases (up/down positions). Counter only increments when full range of motion with correct form is detected.

Step 07

Testing & Refinement

Extensive testing with various body types, lighting conditions, and camera angles. Fine-tuned detection thresholds for accuracy and reliability.

Ongoing

Future Development

Planning to add more exercises, real-time audio feedback, workout tracking history, and social features for friendly competition.
